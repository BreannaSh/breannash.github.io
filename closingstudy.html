Closing Study


2016
Lustre, Glossy, Matt, Silk and Transparent Printed Paper, Epoxy, Wood, Acid Free Paper.
(each piece) 24” x 36”


When you look at your feet perhaps you see something there. Maybe some dirt, a paperclip, a small creature crawling across the vastness of your bedroom floor. What may seem so commonplace and unnoticable is also very specific, to where you are, to how you live, to what kind of room it is. To you.

Enlarging micro-debris and bringing to view the textures, colours and qualities of these small discarded remnants of space, Closing Study, takes these nuisanced materials and raises them to a place of significance as trace and evidence of a space and activity. This Photo Collage series, born of the indoor pool environment at Huron Park Community Centre in Mississauga is an exploration of size, composition and colour through the eyes of a lifeguard during closing hours.
