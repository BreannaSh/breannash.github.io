Apple Crisp


2014
Walnut, Paint
(Varying sizes)

Using the thin script from an old recipe Apple crisp is the product of a program called aspire,
which inputs lines as vectors and outputs patterns. The patterns are specific to the script style,
unique as is the person who wrote them originally. In this piece, a mostly improvised recipe "apple crisp" was used
to make a playful abstraction of segments whose ability to change form and shape parallels its origins.
