First you watch, as I do, then you will try, I will help you like this, you will go and then I will watch


2017
Forton, Steel, Fiberglass
5’ x 4.5’ x 2’


Reflecting on the stretch before a dive and the curve of the body during the motion, this piece uses formal qualities of limbs outstretched and a common torso to create a visual representation of the instant before a dive. The curved metal bars are to draw the eye in the same manner as the half-moon trajectory of the body pre-motion.




That frozen moment,
before the pounce,
before the buzzer,
before the command,
before the reveal.
Deep inhale.
Shaking exhale.
The Mind cannot keep up,
the body will overtake.
Trust now.
Hope you know enough,
trust enough,
the limbs and muscles,
to do the job,
you’ve done 100 times.
Thought takes the back seat,
you prepare for the Move.
But before you move...
stillness in commitment.
Anticipation.
Windup.
Hold up.
Stretch up.
Deep Breath.
Hold up.
Ready.
Ready... Are you Ready?
