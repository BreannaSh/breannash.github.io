It's how you stand


2017
Plaster, shellac, grass, paper, paint, found clothing stand
4’ x 5.5’ x 2’

It’s how you stand

This collaborative piece was part of an experimental exhibition that took place at SHIMS gallery in Bushwick, NY. The Exhibit “The Furniture we are forever rearranging” looked at the object in domestic space and applied installation considerations of how one might arrange a room with large scale sculptural pieces.
The artists worked directly in the space with emphasis on “arrangement” allowing for pieces to share space in ways that are less common with white cube sculptural installation.

It’s how you stand, was a piece made by Breanna Shanahan with contributions from Montreal based Sculptor and ceramicist Élise Provencher.
